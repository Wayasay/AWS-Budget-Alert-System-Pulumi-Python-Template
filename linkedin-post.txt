🚀 Deploy It #5 — Budget Alert System

AWS already lets you set up billing alerts.

Through 6 manual screens you'll forget how to repeat.

Here's a Pulumi template that does it in one command — and lives in your Git repo like everything else should.

---

The scenario:

You're a founder, a dev, or a small team running workloads on AWS.
Everything's fine. Until you open your inbox and see a $600 billing email.

No warning. No heads up. Just a number you weren't expecting.

---

What this template spins up:

✅ SNS Topic — your notification "broadcast channel"
✅ Email Subscription — wires your inbox directly to it
✅ AWS Budget — watches your monthly spend in real time
✅ 3 Alert Thresholds — 80% actual, 100% actual, 100% forecasted

Three lines of config. One command.

pulumi config set monthly_budget_usd 50
pulumi config set alert_email you@yourdomain.com
pulumi up

Done.

---

💡 The one thing to understand:

AWS Budgets is like a smoke detector.

The smoke detector already exists in your building — AWS has the native tooling.
But without one wired into your flat, the alarm only goes off somewhere else.

This template wires the detector directly to your inbox.
And because it's code, every new environment you spin up gets the same detector — automatically.

That's the point of IaC. Not to do the impossible. To make the repeatable actually repeatable.

---

⚠️ One gotcha: after deploying, AWS will send you a confirmation email.
You MUST click it before alerts activate. Don't skip this step.
